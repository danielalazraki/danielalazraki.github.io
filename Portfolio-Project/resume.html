    My History

    I was born on December 11th, 2002 in Santiago, Chile. There, I studied until 2009 in the Hebrew Institute of Santiago Dr. Chaim Weizman. In February of 2010, I moved to Lima,

    Peru where I went to Leon Pinelo School from Second Grade until I graduated from High School in 2019 with an IB Bilingual Diploma (Spanish as mother tongue and English).

    My family

    My family is made up of my father, mother, younger sister and myself. I am proud to say that I grew up in an amazing family, full of values. My parents taught

    me to be educated, put my values in practice, pursue my goals, to continue with my family and the Jewish traditions and most importantly, to be happy.

    My Studies

    This year I am studying in MIT Xpro Full Stack Developer (MERN stack) Professional Certificate and in University of Edinburgh Digital Marketing

    Professional Certificate, both are fully online. By the end of the year I hope to do Aliyah (move to Israel) and continue my life there.

    Hanoar Hatzioni

    Since 2011, I have been participating in a Zionist Jewish Youth Movement of Non-Formal education. 2 years ago I graduated as a leader of it and I have worked in

    many functions such as Education coordinator of the groups of second and third grade, Leader of the fifth grade men group both during whole 2019, Treasurer of a seminar of the movement in Peru during August 2019, Leader coordinator of education on a week long camp of the movement in Peru during december 2019. In February, 2021 we had a continental capacitation seminar that had approx 250 participants where I participated as Lider coordinator of the educational sector. I have been elected to participate in the movement directory as the Treasurer of the whole organization in Peru for 2021 and as the leader of the group that is preparing to be leaders (15-16 years).
